Violence Against Women and Children Awareness

What is Violence Against Women and Children?

Violence against women and children includes physical, sexual, emotional, and psychological abuse. It is a violation of human rights and a serious form of discrimination.

Statistics and Facts

  • 1 in 3 women globally will experience physical or sexual violence in their lifetime.
  • Every year, millions of children are subjected to violence, including physical abuse, neglect, and exploitation.
  • Gender-based violence is a major driver of long-term harm to women and children, affecting their physical and mental health.

How You Can Help

Get Support

If you or someone you know is experiencing violence, please reach out to a local support service or helpline.
